What are PP Bottles?

PP (Polypropylene) bottles are widely used containers known for their durability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. They are resistant to chemicals and suitable for both food and non-food applications.

These bottles are lightweight, recyclable, and provide excellent resistance to stress and cracks, making them a reliable choice across industries.
